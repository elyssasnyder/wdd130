Welcome To Activities

You’re still here? I must’ve done something right then! While looking at the booths and things for sale is nice, you can do that at any old festival! What sets renaissance faire apart from the rest is there are many activities and things to do and see! Take your kids to go see the mermaids or get their faces painted. Maybe go and watch the performers sing and dance or sit back and watch 2 guys on horses hit each other with long sticks! Or if you don’t want to just sit back and watch things head over to one of the blacksmiths and shoot some arrows or head over to the open field area that has nothing but targets and throw the hatchets, that’s a personal favorite. If you’re not an actiony type person that’s fine too. If you just want to sit somewhere and people watch and see all the amazing costumes, I promise you’re not going to be the only one doing so! Regardless of what you choose to do there’s something for everyone and the whole family to enjoy!
